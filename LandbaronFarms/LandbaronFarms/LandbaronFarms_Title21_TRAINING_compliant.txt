Landbaron Farms
Worker Training (§112.21) - Compliance Evidence Document
Date: May 19, 2025
Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence related to Landbaron Farms' worker training program in accordance with 21 CFR §112.21.

Evidence Summary
Requirement
Training Content & Frequency
Annual training conducted March 2025 covered hygiene, cross-contamination prevention, produce handling, and wildlife monitoring. Curriculum mapped to §112.21(a)–(c), including language-appropriate delivery and trainer qualifications.

Requirement
Attendance & Verification
Attendance rate 100% (128/128 workers). Competency verified via quizzes (average score 91%). New hire onboarding includes training prior to starting covered activities.

Requirement
Recordkeeping
All records signed and dated by trainees and trainer. Records retained per §112.161(b) for ≥2 years; prior year records available on request.

Supporting Documentation
1. Training Curriculum & Slides – 2025 edition aligned to FSMA.
2. Attendance Registers & Quiz Results – March–April 2025.
3. Trainer CVs & Qualifications – internal and third-party trainers.

Observations
* Training program meets or exceeds §112.21 requirements.
* Records are complete, signed, and retained according to §112.161.
* No gaps identified; program effective.
