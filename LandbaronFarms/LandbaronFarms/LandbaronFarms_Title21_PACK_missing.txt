Landbaron Farms
Packinghouse Sanitation (§112.141) - Compliance Evidence Document
Date: March 08, 2025
Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team

Purpose
This document identifies missing documentation related to the sanitation of equipment and buildings in the packinghouse under 21 CFR §112.141.

Evidence Summary
Requirement
SOP Availability
The packinghouse sanitation SOP could not be located. No approved version was provided for review.

Requirement
Environmental Monitoring
No environmental swab results available for conveyor belts or packing tables; last report on file dated 2022.

Requirement
Corrective Action Records
No corrective action records provided for cleaning deviations or failed inspections.

Supporting Documentation
1. SOP Request Emails – unanswered.
2. Environmental Testing Requests – pending sample schedule.
3. Maintenance Ticket System – no entries linked to sanitation deviations.

Observations
* Evidence missing to demonstrate compliance with §112.141.
* Absence of SOP and testing records prevents verification of sanitation effectiveness.
* Recommend issuing and training to a current SOP, and implementing routine swab testing with corrective action tracking.
