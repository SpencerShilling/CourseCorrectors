Rolling Meadow Orchard (RMO)
Fixed Asset Maintenance Records Outdated Review
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Financial Compliance and Accounting Team

Purpose
This document identifies outdated evidence within RMO’s fixed asset maintenance files, specifically missing updates to maintenance logs and outdated service histories.

Evidence Summary
Maintenance Log Review —
Quarterly maintenance checks for tractors, pallet loaders, and orchard vehicles were not updated past Q2 FY2024.

Service Record Evaluation —
Vendor service documentation for refrigeration and bottling equipment was last uploaded in 2022.

Asset Lifecycle Review —
Depreciation recalculations tied to maintenance schedules were not updated in accordance with the 2024 policy.

Accuracy Assessment —
Outdated maintenance logs and service histories reduce reliability of asset valuation and lifecycle planning.

Supporting Documentation
    1   Maintenance Log Archive — Entries last updated June 2024.
    2   Vendor Service Folder — Missing FY2023–2024 uploads.
    3   Depreciation Policy Addendum — 2024 requirements.
    4   Equipment Service Matrix — Incomplete updates.

Observations
    •   Missing updates for multiple service cycles.
    •   Outdated logs affect depreciation accuracy.
    •   Maintenance documentation requires immediate update.
