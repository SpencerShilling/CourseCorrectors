Rolling Meadow Orchard (RMO)
OMSS Sales & Adjustments Log Missing Evidence Review
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Financial Compliance and Accounting Team

Purpose
This document identifies missing logs and adjustment records within the Orchard Management and Sales System (OMSS) needed for FY2024 reconciliation.

Evidence Summary
Adjustment Log Review —
Price overrides, spoilage entries, and bulk order adjustments for Q3 and Q4 FY2024 were not present.

Sales Exception Records —
Exception logs documenting corrected transactions were missing for multiple high-volume weekends.

Audit Trail Review —
Audit trails for user edits in the retail store module had no recorded entries for July–September.

Risk Assessment —
Missing logs hinder validation of sales integrity and transaction-level accuracy.

Supporting Documentation
    1   OMSS Audit Extract — Missing adjustment entries.
    2   Exception Log Index — Gaps in record sequence.
    3   Retail Module Procedures — Documentation for overrides.
    4   IT Ticket Log — Requests to regenerate missing audit records.

Observations
    •   Missing adjustment logs for second half of FY2024.
    •   Exception logs incomplete.
    •   Audit trail activity absent for key months.
