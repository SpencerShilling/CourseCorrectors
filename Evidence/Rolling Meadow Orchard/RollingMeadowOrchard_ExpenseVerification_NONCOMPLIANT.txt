Rolling Meadow Orchard (RMO)
Expense Verification Non-Compliance Review
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Financial Compliance and Accounting Team

Purpose
This document identifies evidence of non-compliance in RMO’s FY2024 expense verification process. Findings relate to missing documentation, incomplete approval workflows, and coding inaccuracies.

Evidence Summary
Expense Documentation Review —
Multiple expense entries lacked supporting receipts, including payments to Greenline Equipment Rentals and Harvest Supplies Co.

Authorization Workflow Check —
Two high-value expenses (>$10,000) failed to include required Finance Director approval.

Ledger Coding Validation —
Several travel expenses were misclassified under Orchard Equipment Maintenance, distorting account category totals.

Compliance Impact —
Documentation gaps and approval failures reduce reliability of expense reporting and violate internal control policy.

Supporting Documentation
    1   Expense Ledger Extract — Flagged entries.
    2   Missing Receipt Log — Items lacking documentation.
    3   Policy Reference — Authorization thresholds and documentation requirements.
    4   Approval Audit Report — Unapproved high-value transactions.

Observations
    •   Receipt and approval documentation incomplete.
    •   Misclassified expenses affect category accuracy.
    •   Corrections required prior to audit completion.
