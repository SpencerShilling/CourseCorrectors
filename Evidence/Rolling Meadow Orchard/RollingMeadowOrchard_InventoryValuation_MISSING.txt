Rolling Meadow Orchard (RMO)
Inventory Valuation Missing Evidence Review
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Financial Compliance and Accounting Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence of missing inventory valuation records required for FY2024 financial audit validation. Missing documents relate to physical counts, variance explanations, and supervisory approval.

Evidence Summary
Physical Count Worksheets —
Year-end count sheets for packaged apple products, cider production stock, and retail inventory were not found in OMSS or physical archives.

Variance Analysis Review —
Required shrinkage and spoilage variance reports were not present in audit files.

Approval Record Check —
Supervisor and Operations Manager sign-off sheets for inventory verification were missing.

Risk Assessment —
Missing records limit assurance of valuation accuracy and increase potential misstatement of COGS.

Supporting Documentation
    1   Missing Inventory Report — Items with no FY2024 records.
    2   OMSS Extract — Year-end inventory data gaps.
    3   Inventory Policy — Required count and variance procedures.
    4   Email Request Log — Attempts to retrieve missing documents.

Observations
    •   Physical count sheets and variance reports not located.
    •   Approval documentation incomplete.
    •   Verification process cannot be fully validated.
