Rolling Meadow Orchard (RMO)
Accounts Payable Non-Compliance Review
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Financial Compliance and Accounting Team

Purpose
This document highlights non-compliant findings related to FY2024 accounts payable procedures, including late payments, missing signatures, and incorrect vendor coding.

Evidence Summary
Late Payment Review —
Seven invoices exceeded the 30-day payment window required under vendor contracts.

Signature Verification —
Three payment authorization forms lacked required dual signatures.

Vendor Coding Analysis —
Two major vendors were mapped to outdated ledger codes, impacting AP aging reports.

Compliance Impact —
Late payments and incomplete approvals pose financial and vendor relationship risks.

Supporting Documentation
    1   AP Aging Report — Overdue items.
    2   Vendor Contract Extract — Payment terms.
    3   Signature Audit Log — Missing authorization.
    4   GL Code Map — Incorrect vendor mapping.

Observations
    •   Multiple late payments.
    •   Missing signature approvals.
    •   Coding errors affecting AP accuracy.
