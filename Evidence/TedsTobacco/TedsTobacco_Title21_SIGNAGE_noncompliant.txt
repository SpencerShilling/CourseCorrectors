Ted’s Tobacco
Point-of-Sale Signage Audit
Date: May 26, 2025
Prepared by: Retail Compliance and Audit Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence of noncompliance with signage requirements under 21 CFR §1140.30(a)(2). The regulation mandates that each point of sale display a clear and visible sign stating that sales to individuals under 21 years of age are prohibited.

Evidence Summary
Store Inspections —
Three retail outlets (Stores #18, #42, #58) lacked proper “No Sale Under 21” signage.

Visibility Assessment —
At Store #18, signage was faded and illegible. Store #58 had no signage posted at all.

Maintenance Logs —
Records showed no replacement or inspection entries since 2022.

Compliance Verification —
In-store photos confirmed absence or deterioration of required signs.

Corrective Actions —
Replacement signage ordered, pending distribution by regional facilities.

Supporting Documentation

Inspection Photos — Visual documentation of missing or faded signage.

Maintenance Logs — Store-level records with last update entries from 2022.

Corrective Tickets — Work orders for replacement signage installation.

Regional Compliance Summary — Confirmation of pending signage shipments.

Observations

Required signage missing or unreadable in three retail locations.

Lack of maintenance records indicates inadequate compliance tracking.

Signage replacement and periodic verification required within 14 days.
