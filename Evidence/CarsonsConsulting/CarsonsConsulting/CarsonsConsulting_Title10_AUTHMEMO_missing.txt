Carson’s Consulting Delegation Authorization Memo Review Date: June 19, 2025 Prepared by: Legal Compliance and Administrative Oversight Team
Purpose This document provides evidence of missing delegation authorization memos required under 10 CFR §13.25. These memos verify that reviewing officials were granted authority to issue complaints before settlement execution.
Evidence Summary Authorization Audit — Four settlement case files lacked signed delegation memos confirming approval authority.
Record System Review — LDMS log entries contained placeholders for delegation documentation with no uploaded attachments.
Risk Evaluation — Missing authorization records reduce assurance of procedural compliance and expose the company to potential review challenges.
Remedial Measures — Retroactive delegation memos requested from Legal Affairs with submission deadline July 10, 2025.
