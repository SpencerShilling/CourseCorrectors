Tobacco Hut Junior Point-of-Sale Signage Violations Date: May 19, 2025 Prepared by: Retail Compliance and Audit Team
Purpose This document provides evidence of noncompliance with 21 CFR §1140.30(a)(2), which requires that retailers display visible signage stating that the sale of tobacco products to individuals under 21 years of age is prohibited.
Evidence Summary Audit Scope — Store inspections were conducted across 10 Tobacco Hut Junior retail locations during May 2025.
Visibility Review — Three stores were found without visible “No Sale Under 21” signage at all registers, and two stores had signs that were faded or obstructed from view.
Maintenance Review — Signage maintenance logs have not been updated since 2023, and no procedures exist for monthly verification.
Photographic Documentation — Audit photos confirmed missing signage at Stores #104, #119, and #121, and faded posters at #112 and #116.
Compliance Notice — A corporate notice was issued on May 20, 2025, requiring replacement and standard placement of signage by June 1, 2025.
Supporting Documentation
	1	Store Inspection Reports — Detailing signage conditions and deficiencies.
	2	Maintenance Logs — Records showing no updates since 2023.
	3	Photographic Evidence — Dated images confirming signage absence.
	4	Corrective Action Notice — Corporate directive for signage replacement.
Observations
	•	Missing or unreadable signage at five store locations.
	•	Outdated maintenance procedures failed to ensure visibility compliance.
	•	Corrective verification must be completed within 14 days.
