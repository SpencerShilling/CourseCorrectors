Terry’s Agriculture Microbial Quality Criterion Compliance Audit Date: June 18, 2025 Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team
Purpose This document provides evidence that Terry’s Agriculture’s irrigation and post-harvest water meets microbial quality criteria established under 21 CFR Part 112. The audit confirms ongoing testing and verification protocols ensure safety for produce consumption.
Evidence Summary Testing Frequency — Bi-weekly microbial tests conducted on all active irrigation systems and post-harvest wash stations.
Results — All test samples reported less than 126 CFU/100 mL geometric mean for E. coli, and no single sample exceeded 410 CFU/100 mL.
Compliance Monitoring — Microbial testing procedures aligned with FSMA standards and verified by third-party accredited laboratories.
System Logging — Results uploaded automatically to the AWMS, ensuring traceability and secure recordkeeping.
Supporting Documentation
	1	Microbial Analysis Reports — Certified lab data, April–June 2025.
	2	AWMS System Export — Verified results and timestamps.
	3	QA Review Logs — Quality assurance sign-offs for all samples.
	4	FSMA Compliance Certificate — Annual microbial compliance verification.
Observations
	•	All microbial results within compliant thresholds.
	•	Verification logs confirm complete traceability and oversight.
	•	No corrective actions required.
