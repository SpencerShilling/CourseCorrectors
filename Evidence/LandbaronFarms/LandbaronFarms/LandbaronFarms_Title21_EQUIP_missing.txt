Landbaron Farms
Equipment, Tools, and Buildings – Cleaning (§112.123) - Compliance Evidence Document
Date: September 12, 2025
Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence gaps related to the cleaning and sanitizing of food-contact surfaces under 21 CFR §112.123 and §112.128.

Evidence Summary
Requirement
Sanitation Logs
No cleaning or sanitizer concentration logs found for harvest totes, knives, and sorting tables during July–September 2025.

Requirement
Sanitizer Validation
No records verifying sanitizer meets EPA food-contact requirements (40 CFR 180.940).

Requirement
Pre-Operation Inspections
Pre-op inspection checklists were requested but not provided; no photographic evidence available.

Supporting Documentation
1. Record Requests – unanswered as of report date.
2. SOP Index – entries for tool sanitation missing.
3. Staff Interviews – verbal confirmation of weekly cleaning without documentation.

Observations
* Insufficient evidence to verify compliance with §112.123 and §112.128.
* Absence of records prevents demonstrating ongoing sanitation control.
* Recommend immediate restoration of documentation and daily pre-op inspections with supervisor sign-off.
