Landbaron Farms
Biological Soil Amendments (§112.51–§112.60) - Compliance Evidence Document
Date: March 30, 2025
Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence of noncompliance with biological soil amendment treatment and application requirements under 21 CFR §112.54 and §112.56.

Evidence Summary
Requirement
Compost Process Validation
Windrow compost logs recorded temperatures between 43–48 C; required is >=55 C for at least 15 days with 5 turnings (or static pile >=55 C for >=3 days) per §112.54(b). Turning records missing for 4 consecutive days (June 10–13, 2025).

Requirement
Untreated Manure Interval
Untreated manure applied on March 3, 2025; harvest occurred July 25, 2025 (~144 days). Minimum interval of 9 months required when using untreated soil amendments in a manner where they may contact produce (§112.56).

Requirement
Cross-Contamination Controls
Equipment used for manure application not segregated from harvest tools; no cleaning logs to demonstrate sanitation prior to produce contact.

Supporting Documentation
1. Compost Temperature & Turning Logs – Feb–Jun 2025.
2. Fertilizer Application Records & Harvest Logs – 2025 season.
3. Equipment Cleaning SOP – not implemented for manure-contact tools.

Observations
* Treatment parameters below required thresholds (§112.54).
* Harvest interval insufficient for untreated manure (§112.56).
* Immediate corrective actions: discontinue untreated manure use or validate treatment; implement equipment segregation and sanitation verification.
