Landbaron Farms
Wildlife Monitoring (§112.83) - Compliance Evidence Document
Date: October 22, 2025
Prepared by: Agricultural Quality and Compliance Team

Purpose
This document provides evidence that wildlife monitoring procedures are outdated and insufficiently enforced under 21 CFR §112.83.

Evidence Summary
Requirement
SOP Version
Wildlife monitoring SOP last updated in 2019; does not include current buffer guidance or criteria for evaluating intrusions at harvest.

Requirement
Training Currency
No refresher training in 2025; last training in 2021. Patrol coverage decreased in 2025 season due to staff turnover.

Requirement
Patrol Documentation
Patrol logs missing for multiple weeks during harvest; no GPS-tagged routes or photographic evidence retained.

Supporting Documentation
1. Wildlife Monitoring SOP – Rev 1.2 (2019).
2. Training Records – last completion 2021.
3. Patrol Logs – incomplete 2025 entries.

Observations
* Procedures and training are outdated; documentation insufficient to ensure risk-based decisions.
* Recommend updating SOP to include explicit buffer, flagging, and disposition criteria; resume routine training with documented patrols.
