Zuul Tobacco Rules

No Retailer shall sell Tobacco products to anyone under the age of 18

Each retailer must verify via photographic ID that customers are over the age of 18

No such verification is required for any over the age of 26

Tobacco products must be sold in direct face-to-face exchanges only

Tobacco Products cannot be opened or broken to sell individual cigarettes or any number of cigarettes below the minimum package size.
